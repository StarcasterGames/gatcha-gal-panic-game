Gatcha Gal Panic

An otaku love letter: conquer claw machines, recruit AI Gals, and build the ultimate café franchise in Neo‑Akiba. Fully SFW, brimming with style.

Play Demo View Design Document

Features

Jump into a slice of Neo‑Akiba. Explore a small open world, pop into arcades inspired by Akiba landmarks, meet Uka Torigoe in your café and embark on missions. Practice your claw skills across three machine types and start collecting your Gals.

What’s New in v0.6?

Our first gameplay prototype now features a full overworld map with 5 distinct arcades, each with their own themes and signage. Missions from Uka now grant Yen and café décor upgrades, and your progress is tracked in an on‑screen HUD. Three machine types are playable: Loose Girls (softbody), Capsules (rigid) and Bridge‑Style bar drop. More modes are on the roadmap!
